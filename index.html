How it started

We made this for our mother first.

When our mother passed away in May 2026, we wanted to honour her properly — not just with a funeral notice, but with something that felt like her. A site that told people where to come, what to wear, and what she meant to us. A book that captured the memories of everyone who loved her.

We built it ourselves. And then we thought: other families should have this too.
